5 Fun Facts About Boba

1. It came from Taiwan

Boba tea, also known as bubble tea, originated in Taiwan in the 1980s.

2. "Boba"s are Tapicoia pearls

The name "boba" refers to the chewy tapioca pearls found at the bottom of the drink.

3. It can be hot or cold

Boba tea can be served hot or cold, and cold versions are usually blended with ice.
